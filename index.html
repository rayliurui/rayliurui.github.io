Today IP drama accounts for a huge part in people's entertainment choices.

IP drama which refers to drama adapted from novels, games and animates as well as drama being remade, is not a new term, but popular again due to the increasing IP market.

Chinese IP Drama in 2018

Process

We processed the data according to the following steps:

  1. Collecting from National Radio and Television Administration (NRTA) webisite and Douban.com
  2. Cleaning
  3. Transforming and summarizing

Content

It is of people's interest and need to make a data analysis on Chinese IP drama in recent years. We focus on two aspects:

  • Quantity
  • Quality

Quantity

Though the number of all drama got down, IP drama plays an increasingly huge part

Quality

Online IP drama also has excellent work as TV drama. The common problem is that the number of excellent work is still small and the quality of IP drama needs to be improved.
